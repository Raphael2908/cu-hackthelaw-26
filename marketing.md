# Legal Drafting Copilot — Go-To-Market Plan: First 10 Design-Partner Lawyers

> Companion docs: `architecture.md` (design), `current_progress.md` (build log), `todo.md` (backlog).
> This file is the **marketing** source of truth. Built for a hackathon → early design-partner stage.

## Context

**Where we are:** pre-launch, hackathon scaffold. No audience, no budget. The goal is the **first ~10
design-partner lawyers** who will run a real brief and tell us whether the synthesised argument + evidence
triage saves them time. This is qualitative validation, not a revenue milestone yet.

**The unfair advantage:** the output *is* the demo — a synthesised, cited argument for a real legal task
(e.g. an M&A first-draft plan) is itself the most persuasive marketing artefact. One good run = one
shareable proof.

**Honest constraints to respect:**
- Lawyers are conservative buyers and sensitive about confidentiality — lead with "you stay in control"
  (you set N, you review the flagged evidence, nothing is auto-filed).
- **Lead with what ships today:** plan → research (web + CELLAR) → rank → evaluate top-N → synthesise.
  The Phase-2 clause-level document-graph review is a "coming soon" hook, not the pitch.
- EU-law grounding via CELLAR is a concrete differentiator vs generic AI drafting — emphasise it.

## The strategy at a glance

1. **Direct outreach to a narrow ICP** — founder-led, hand-to-hand. The source of the first 10.
2. **Dogfood proof artefacts** — run real (anonymised) briefs, share the output as the demo.
3. No paid spend at this stage.

## CHANNEL 1 — Direct outreach (the first-10 engine)

### ICP (narrow)
EU/UK corporate & M&A associates and small-firm partners who (a) draft first-pass plans/memos under time
pressure, (b) work with EU legal sources, (c) can say yes to a pilot without procurement.

| Segment | Why they fit | Where to find them |
|---|---|---|
| Boutique M&A / corporate firms (EU) | high draft volume, no big-firm AI tooling, fast decisions | LinkedIn, legal-tech meetups, alumni networks |
| In-house counsel at scale-ups | wear many hats, value speed, EU-law exposure | founder networks, legal-ops communities |

### The offer that converts cold
"Send me one real (or representative) brief; I'll return a synthesised argument with ranked, EU-source-
cited evidence and the points flagged for your review — free, no signup." Deliver the aha before any ask.

### Cadence
~10 personalised outreaches/day. Track each in a sheet (name, segment, channel, sent, replied, ran a
brief, feedback).

## CHANNEL 2 — Dogfood proof artefacts
Run real anonymised briefs through the product; publish the (sanitised) outputs as case studies and
short LinkedIn posts: "here's the EU-grounded first-draft plan this produced in N minutes."

## Conversion
- **Deliver the aha early** — run their brief for them before they sign up.
- **Confidentiality story up front** — data handling, no training on their docs.
- **Keep them in control** — they set N and review flagged evidence; the tool drafts, they decide.

## Success metrics
- **North star:** 10 lawyers who ran a real brief and gave structured feedback.
- **Funnel:** outreach → replied → ran a brief → would use again / would pay.
